LIST OF BREEDS
DOBERMAN
A medium-large breed of domestic dog that was originally developed around 1890 by Louis Dobermann, a tax collector from Germany.
GREAT DANE
Descends from hunting dogs from the Middle Ages used to hunt wild boar and deer, and as guardians of German nobility. It is one of the largest breeds in the world along with its relative, the Irish Wolfhound.
HUSKY
A general term for a dog used in the polar regions, primarily and specifically for work as sled dogs. It refers to a traditional northern type, notable for its cold-weather tolerance and overall hardiness.
SHIBA INU
A small, alert, and agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain and hiking trails, that is native to Japan. They are considered the oldest and smallest of Japan's dogs.
PUG
A breed of dog distinguished by a wrinkly, short-muzzled face and curled tail. They are one of the oldest breeds of dog; has flourished since before 400 BC.
CORGI
A small to medium-sized herding dog of short stature and sturdy build, known for its low-set body, large erect ears, and stubby tail.
